## T1: Standard Combo
### Required Pieces
- Radiant Typhoon Mandate placed face-up in spell/trap zone
- Radiant Typhoon Ascendance set in spell/trap zone
- The three remaining spell/trap zones should be filled out with as many set copies of Mystical Space Typhoon (minimum 1x Mystical Space Typhoon, preferably 2x) and/or set copies of Non-Engine spell/trap cards (preferbly 1x) as available
- Radiant Typhoon Varuroon, the Marine Eidolon present in the Extra Monster Zone
- Radiant Typhoon Varuroon, the Vibrant Vortex present in monster zone
- One copy of Mystical Space Typhoon in the GY
### Optional Pieces
- Radiant Typhoon Meghala present in monster zone
- Radiant Typhoon Fonix, the Great Flame present in either hand, monster zone, or GY
- Totem bird present in monster zone
### Annotations (Agnes)
- This is the basic end-board of the Radiant Typhoon deck that should be reachable with most hands that include a starter. However, in some one-card combos Radiant Typhoon Varuroon, the Vibrant Vortex might be unable to be reached. The optional pieces strenghten the board but often requires more engine cards to reach.
